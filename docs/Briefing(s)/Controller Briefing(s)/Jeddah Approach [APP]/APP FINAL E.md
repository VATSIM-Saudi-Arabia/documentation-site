## Notes
### Arrival
- 1500-6500ft = **Class C airspace**
- **RWY34R** for arrivals.
- There is a large restricted zone in the **East** of Jeddah TMA, *Makkah.*
- Arrivals for **RWY34R** will be handed off the **Final East** at **PUSPO** passing **7000 ft**.
- Aircraft must be given **4500** at the sequencing leg to avoid MRVA of **4200**.
- For **ILS Y** (IAF ITOMA) descend to **3000ft** at ITOMA
- Suggested Vectors: **250** (Base), **310** (Intercepting Vector)
- Handoff to OEJN_E_TWR for **RWY34R**
- REFER TO JEDDAH APP SOP FOR FURTHER

!!! danger "DO NOTE"
    **RWY34R** is generally considered the **HIGH** side, hence aircraft must be given the instruction to **descend 4000ft** whilst intercepting the localizer.
